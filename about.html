Overview

Project Blackbird is a python-driven project that analyzes Call of Duty vods by quickly parsing each frame to read scores, track player positions, and dynamically determine spawns, hardpoints, etc. Spectator mode is used to more accurately determine movement on the minimap, and is required for any VODs uploaded requiring the minimap (such as Killfeed and Spawns). At this time, processing just the scores also requires spectator mode, as it's quicker and much more accurate than using regular gameplay footage.

How It Works

When you upload a video to the site, our python backend processes the video frame-by-frame, and extracts information which is then given to you by CSV or uploaded to our database for you to access.
