About

Upload a URL to a live or recorded Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War clip and get access to data that can be analyzed and reviewed to help improve your team's game. Track scores based on hard points, analyze the killfeed automatically, and track player movement throughout the game.

Types of Data

  • Scores

    For each map, get the score at every second to provide analysis on rotations and hardpoints.

  • Killfeed

    Instead of transcribing the killfeed by hand, get the time of each kill thoughout the game.

  • Player Locations and Spawns

    Track player movement across the minimap to determine death locations, spawn locations, and the locations of players at every kill.

Purpose

Personalized post-match data can be useful for all teams, whether or not you're in CDL. Give your team the edge with detailed spawn information, including where friendly and enemy players are at each spawn, to better understand where and why certain spawns are selected. Detailed score information can give you a better sense for how hardpoints should be used and when rotations make the most sense. Map-by-map break downs can help show flaws in your team's strategies, especially when tracking your performance against specific teams.
